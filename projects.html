GitHub Projects

  • Favorite Recept

    A recipe management platform where users can save, organize, and explore their favorite recipes. It includes features for categorizing recipes by type, searching for specific ingredients, and providing cooking instructions.

  • Happy Dogs App

    An app designed for dog lovers, where users can find dog-friendly parks, access dog care tips, and connect with local dog events. The app emphasizes community and healthy, happy lifestyles for dogs.

  • Mobile Repair Shop

    A digital platform tailored for mobile repair businesses. It allows customers to schedule repair appointments, track repair status, and receive notifications. The app also helps businesses manage inventory and customer data.

  • Online Butikplatform

    An e-commerce platform supporting online boutiques. The project includes product listings, a shopping cart, secure checkout, and customer reviews. It's designed for boutique owners to easily manage their products and customers.

  • Restaurant Billing System

    A system for streamlining billing operations in restaurants. It includes order processing, bill generation, and payment tracking features. It aims to simplify restaurant management and improve the customer experience.
