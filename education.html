Axelos global practice (ITIL Foundation 4, 2021)

The ITIL Foundation 4 course at Axelos covers the fundamentals of ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library), the globally recognized framework for IT service management (ITSM). Designed to enhance efficiency and align IT services with business objectives, this course introduces the foundational principles and practices of ITIL 4, including the Service Value System (SVS) and the four dimensions of service management. ITIL 4 represents a significant update to previous ITIL versions, emphasizing an agile, collaborative, and holistic approach to ITSM. The course focuses on key components, such as value creation through effective service delivery, adaptability in fast-changing digital environments, and best practices for continuous improvement. Participants gain a comprehensive understanding of ITIL 4 concepts, such as the ITIL guiding principles, governance, service value chain, and continual improvement.
